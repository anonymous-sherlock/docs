## Planned Features

These are the features that we've planned to work on in the near future.

### AI Integration

Incorporate artificial intelligence to enhance CRM functionalities, providing intelligent insights and automation.

- [GitHub Issue #1](https://github.com/adscrushcrm/adscrush-crm/issues/1)

### Traffic Source Integrations

Integrate with various traffic sources to streamline lead tracking and campaign management, ensuring a comprehensive view of your marketing efforts.

- [GitHub Issue #2](https://github.com/adscrushcrm/adscrush-crm/issues/2)

### Predictive Analytics

Implement predictive analytics to help businesses anticipate customer behavior and make data-driven decisions.

- [GitHub Issue #3](https://github.com/adscrushcrm/adscrush-crm/issues/3)
