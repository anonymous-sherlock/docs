# Create Product

Welcome to the Create Product documentation for AdCrush. This guide will walk you through the process creating a product using our platform.

## Introduction

Creating a product on AdCrush is a simple and straightforward process. With just a few clicks, you'll be able to create a new product and start managing it right away.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have the following:

- A valid AdCrush account
- The necessary permissions to create products

## Step-by-Step Guide

**Navigate to :** [`https://affilate.adscrush.com/products/create`](https://affilate.adscrush.com/products/create)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your AdCrush account">
    Log in with your AdCrush account credentials.
    <Frame>
  <img src="/images/step-1.png" />
</Frame>

  </Step>
  <Step title={`Navigate to the "Products" section`}>
    Once logged in, navigate to the 'Products' section in the AdCrush dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title={`Click the "New Product" button`}>
    In the `Products` dropdown section, locate and click the `New Product` button.
  </Step>
  <Step title={`Fill the product details`}>
    Complete the required fields and provide details for your new product.

| Fields              | Description                                                                                    |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Product Name        | Name of the product                                                                            |
| Product Price       | Reported price of the product                                                                  |
| Product Category    | Category to which the product belongs                                                          |
| Product Quantity    | Quantity available or associated with the product                                              |
| Product Description | Detailed description of the product                                                            |
| Media URLs          | URLs of media content related to the product                                                   |
| Product Images      | Images representing the product supported images type `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`, `.webp`, `.svg` |

<Warning><b>Important:</b> you can upload max upto 10 images with 5MB each file.</Warning>

  </Step>
  <Step title={`Click "Add Product" to create your product`}>
    These are instructions or content that only pertain to the third step.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Conclusion

Congratulations! You have successfully created a product on AdCrush. You can now start managing your product and taking advantage of all the features our platform has to offer.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to contact our support team.
